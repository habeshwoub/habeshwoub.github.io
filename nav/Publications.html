Publications

So far, I have authored and co-authored 12 articles in scientifc journals and conferences. You can find my work in software engineering journals and conferences (e.g., Information and software technology, Software quality Journal, PROFES, EASE,etc. ). More in the pipeline 😜



Towards optimal quality requirement documentation in agile software development

This work is accepted in the special issue of "Recent Trends in Engineering Software-Intensive Systems", in the Journal of Systems and software. It examines practitioners viewpoint of Quality requirement documentation in agile software development. It also introduces an initial mode for supporting quality requirement documentation in agile software development..

Technical debt in the context of agile software development

This work is published in the Journal of Information and software technology and it synthesizes knowledge on the state of the art of the management of technical debt in the context of agile software development. A systematic literature review is used as a research method.

Quality requirement in agile and rapid software development - state of the art

Another one of my works that is pubished in the Journal of Information and software technology. The study structures and synthesizes the body of knowledge of the literature of management of Quality requirements in agile and rapid softwaer development.

Quality requirement documentation in agile software development

This work is published in the Evaluation and Assessment in Software Engineering EASE 2020, conference. It presents the state of the practice of QR documentation in agile software development companies.

Non-functional requirement documentation in agile softwaer development

This work was published in PROFES 2017.It presents the findings on non-functional requirement documentation practices and challenges in agile companies. Additionally, it introduces a guidelines for enhancing NFRS documentation in agile software development.
