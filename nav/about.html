A little about me

I am Woubshet-Behutiye and i am doing software engineering research @ M3S - software engineering research unit of the university of Oulu. My research interest covers software development processes, agile software development, requirements engineering, technical debt, software quality and AI. I have more than five years of experience in software engineering reserch. In my work I collaborate with researchers from the software industry and academic institutes. I have published and presented my work in scientifc journals and conferences. You can find my work in software engineering journals and conferences (e.g., Information and software technology, Software quality Journal, PROFES, EASE,etc. ).
